About the exam...
JEE is one of the toughest enginerring entrance examinations conducted in India. National Eligibility cum Entrance Test is conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA) for admission to undergraduate (MBBS/BDS/Ayush Courses) every year. As per Government of India, it is a mandated requirement to qualify JEE Exam to study medical courses in India and abroad.

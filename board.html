About the exam...
All India Senior School Certificate Examination also called Boards in common language, is an examination conducted every year by the Central Board of Secondary Education on behalf of the Government of India.Each subject in the examination is graded out of 100 marks. For subjects like physics, chemistry, mathematics, biology, etc. in which practical exams are also conducted, 30/20 marks are reserved for practicals and internals and 70/80 marks for the theory exams.
